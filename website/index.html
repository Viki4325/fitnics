BMR Calculator

Fitnics features a calculator for BMR, Basal Metabolic Rate, that is a minimum number of calories required for basic functions at rest. Users can get their BMR calculated based on the information entered. As time passes and users gain progress towards better health, they can adjust their personal information to keep BMR up-to-date. This feature is very useful for those wanting to base their fitness journey on updated data.

Activity Levels

Fitnics has four activity levels. "Not Active" means that you get no formal exercise and are not physically active during the day. "Somewhat Active" refers to activities you do regularly as a part of your everyday life. "Active" refers to participating in some type of cardiorespiratory endurance exercise for at 20 to 60 minutes, three to five days per week. "Very Active" means that you exercise for 20 to 60 minutes most days a week. Fitnics already adjust your daily caloric allowance according to your physical activiy level. This prevents under or over consumption of food. This feature is great for those feeling tired of never-ending calculations and logging.

Adjustable Fitness Goals

Fitnics has three fitness goals for you to choose from. "Lose Weight" and "Gain Weight" are for those who are a little conscious about their health and weight. "Maintain Weight" is for those feeling great about the state of their health and weight and want to maintain it. Fitnics takes your unique fitness goal into consideration while calculating your daily caloric allowance.
